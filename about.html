The Founder

Built one brand
at a time.

Asia Holdings didn't start as a plan to own six brands. It started with one storefront, a willingness to learn retail from the ground up, and a habit of reinvesting every rupee of profit back into the next idea.

Motive

Our Organization's vision

To grow alongside our customers through consistent excellence and unwavering integrity. We aim to master every discipline we undertake, turning every interaction into a foundation of lasting trust.

The first holding was Asia Traders, built around a simple insight: people upgrade their phones constantly, but the accessories market serving them was scattered and inconsistent. Getting that right — sourcing, pricing, and customer trust — became the template for everything that followed.

From there, the portfolio grew toward what people actually asked for: gifts that felt personal, not mass-produced. Asia Store and The Customizer both answer that demand, each with its own catalogue and customer base, while Eager Custom took the same idea of personalization global through a dedicated jewellery line sold internationally.

Scents by Asia is the newest chapter — a fragrance line built on the same principle as everything else here: control the product, know the customer, and don't outsource what you can do better in-house.

Asia Digital exists because running five brands meant learning Shopify, paid social, and marketing strategy out of necessity. Packaging that knowledge for other businesses was the natural next step.

Operating Principles

What runs through every brand.

01

Own the Craft

Every brand here is built in-house — product, design, and customer experience are never fully outsourced.

02

Reinvest, Always

Each new brand has been funded by the one before it — growth is earned, not borrowed.

03

Know the Customer

From mobile accessories to custom jewellery, every product decision starts with what the customer is actually asking for.
